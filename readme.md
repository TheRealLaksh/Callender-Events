Calibridge is a premium, high-utility tool designed to simplify the creation, management, and export of calendar events. It generates industry-standard **.ics files** that are universally compatible with major calendar services like **Google Calendar, Outlook, Notion, and Apple Calendar (iCal)**, ensuring your scheduling is reliable and consistent across all platforms.

## ‚ú® Key Features

* **Universal Compatibility:** Exports `.ics` files with explicit **Time Zone** and **Alarm** (`VALARM`) settings for maximum fidelity across different calendar clients.

* **Precision Scheduling:** Dedicated inputs for **Start Time**, **End Time**, **Location**, and customizable **Multiple Reminders** (Minutes, Hours, Days).

* **Time Zone Support:** Allows selection of common time zones to ensure events are displayed correctly for global coordination.

* **Batch Management:** Features include **Import (.ics)**, **Export (.ics)** with custom file naming, **Event Duplication**, and **Clear All** actions.

* **Premium UX:** Fully responsive dark-theme design optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop, featuring subtle entry and hover animations.

## üöÄ Quick Guide: Bridge Your Calendar in 4 Steps

1. **Add Your Events:** Fill out the form fields (Name, Dates, Reminders, etc.) and click **Add Event**.

2. **Export the File:** Click the **Export** button to download your customized **.ics** file.

3. **Open Your External App:** Navigate to the calendar service you want to update (Google Calendar, Outlook, Notion, etc.).

4. **Import the Data:** Find the 'Import' function within your chosen app, attach the downloaded **.ics** file, and you're set!
